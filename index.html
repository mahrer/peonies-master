About Us

Elaine's Peony Gardens is a small, family owned busines in that grows beautiful peonies in a wide variety of colors. Our flowers are among the best in quality and selection, and most importantly, they are home grown with love and care.

Our peonies are at their brightest from mid-May to June. Whether you need a peonies to bighten up your home, sell at your business, or beautify your wedding, Elaine's Peony Gardens will satisfy your needs.

We serve Troutdale, Gresham, Corbett, and East Portland.
